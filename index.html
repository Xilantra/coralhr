Productivity. Tranquility.

Engineering in essence is creating the value, and reengineering is maximizing the value either in accordance to producer or user. The value according to the producer is the profitability and for the user satisfaction and benefit. HR reengineering is not an exception; it is a planned and programmed effort to maximize the value of HR. The benefits are;

To achieve maximum productivity & profitability for the organization
To achieve maximum level of career satisfaction for the individual himself
To restructuring and recreating knowledge and skills of Human Capital, and activate its usage to the organization benefit and converting it to mental capital able to enhance and develop organizational & competitive power of the organization
To redesigning tool & techniques used in Human Capital redesigning (choosing, training, career development, and redesigning principles, fundamentals and processes according to which HR management is applied in the organization)
